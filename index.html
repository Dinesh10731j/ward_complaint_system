About the System

The Ward Complaint System is a platform that allows citizens to submit complaints regarding civic issues like sanitation, water supply, road repairs, and public services. It promotes transparency, efficiency, and accountability in local governance by connecting citizens with ward authorities directly.

Submit Complaints Online

Quickly report civic issues from anywhere using our platform. Track status and stay informed.

Track Complaint Status

Monitor your complaints in real-time and receive notifications about progress and resolution.

Transparent Governance

Ward staff and admins can manage complaints efficiently while citizens stay informed about outcomes.

Role-Based Access

Citizens submit complaints, ward staff process them, and admins oversee the entire system securely.
